Our Family Story

Preserving Nepali culinary heritage since 2006

It all began in a small kitchen in Kathmandu, where Mrs. Laxmi Acharya started making pickles using her grandmother's recipes. What started as a humble family tradition soon captured the hearts of neighbors and friends who couldn't get enough of her authentic Nepali flavors.

Today, Acharya Foods remains a family-run business, with three generations working together to preserve the traditional methods. We still hand-select every ingredient, follow the same time-tested recipes, and pour the same care into every jar that leaves our kitchen.
